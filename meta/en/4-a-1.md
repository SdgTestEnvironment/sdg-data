In Germany, education policy and therefore also the administration of schools and universities is not organized centrally, but is the responsibility of the Ministries of Education and Cultural Affairs of the 16 federal states. The respective <a href="https://www.kmk.org/fileadmin/pdf/PresseUndAktuelles/2002/modernisierung12.pdf" _target="blank" onclick="return confirm_alert('the KMK', 'En');">school construction guidelines and school construction funding guidelines of the federal states</a> apply.
